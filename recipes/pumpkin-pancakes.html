Pumpkin Pancakes

I'll let you in on a secret. Pancakes are my favourite breakfast food. If they aren't yours, let me change your mind with this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup/120g Buckwheat Flour
  • 1 cup/90g Porridge Oats
  • 1 cup/120g Roasted Pumpkin Flesh
  • 1 & 1/2 cup/325ml Non Dairy Milk
  • 4 tbs Maple Syrup
  • Pinch of Sea Salt
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 3 tbs Chia Seeds
  • 2 Scoops Nuzest Clean Lean Protein Powder
  • 1 tbs Coconut Oil for frying

To serve:

  • Nuts
  • Berries
  • Maple Syrup
  • Seeds

Steps

  1. I usually roast a pumpkin or squash the day before if I know I'm making these. The mix works better when the pumpkin is cold.
  2. First up, mash the pumpkin flesh in a mixing bowl then add all the rest of the ingredients & mix together to form a slightly thick batter.
  3. Pre heat a non stick pan over a very low heat and lightly grease with the coconut oil.
  4. When the pan is hot, ladle in the batter. I usually make 3 inch pancakes which is around 5 tbs worth of batter.
  5. Cook for 3-4 minutes on each side.
  6. Between cooking each pancake carefully grease the pan with coconut oil to stop them from sticking. I use kitchen paper to rub the oil into the pan.
  7. Once you've cooked all the pancakes, stack them up & serve with you choice of garnishes.
